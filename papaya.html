Papaya



Papaya is a tropical, nutrient-dense fruit (Carica papaya) known for its sweet, vibrant orange-red flesh and black, peppery edible seeds. Rich in vitamins A and C, it provides significant immune support and antioxidants. It contains the digestive enzyme papain, which helps break down proteins and aids digestion
